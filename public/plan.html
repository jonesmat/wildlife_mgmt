Part IV. Management Plan Goals and Objectives
Goals — what you want the property to look like or what you want to be able to do with it.
Objectives — specific, measurable actions you will take to achieve those goals.
Example: "Goal: Maintain a healthy white-tailed deer population at 1 deer per 30 acres with a 1:1 sex ratio. Objective: Implement a trophy deer management program harvesting 3 does and 2 spike bucks annually, maintain 4 protein feeders year-round, and conduct 3 spotlight surveys each fall."
